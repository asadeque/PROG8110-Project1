Menu - Bi Bim Bap

Bi Bim Bap is a very traditional Korean dish that starts with rice on the bottom and is topped with various vegetables, small amount of beef (should be a similar quantity to the veg) and a fried egg that are individually cooked or seasoned to bring out their best flavors. The dish is served at room temperature with a small amount of Seasoned GoChuJang (Korean Hot Sauce).
